Order Processing

We know you need your gear fast. Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST (Monday - Friday) are processed and shipped the exact same day.

Orders placed after the cutoff, on weekends, or during federal holidays will be processed the following business day. You will receive a tracking link via email the moment your shipping label is printed.

Global Shipping

Core Store proudly ships to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are dynamically calculated at checkout based on your cart weight and destination.

Important Note

Customs duties, taxes, and import fees are not included in the checkout price and are the sole responsibility of the customer upon arrival.
